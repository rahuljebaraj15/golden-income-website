What is Forex Trading?

Forex, also known as foreign exchange or FX trading, is the conversion of one currency into another. It is one of the most actively traded markets in the world, with an average daily trading volume of $6.6 trillion. People trade foreign exchange for many reasons, including profit, international trade, and tourism.

At Golden Income, we provide the knowledge and tools to help you understand and succeed in this dynamic market.

Key Advantages of Forex Trading:

  • 24/5 Market: Trade almost anytime, anywhere.
  • High Liquidity: Easy to enter and exit trades.
  • Leverage: Potential for higher returns with smaller capital (comes with higher risk).
  • Diversification: A unique asset class distinct from stocks.
